About Our Cookies


You may wonder how we stumbled into making salmon cookies to share with the world. In 1998 we were on a vision quest on the west coast of Alaska. It was there that we met a man named Qizhjeh. He was a simple soul living off the land and harvesting food from his natural surroundings Qizhjeh at a log of fish. It was here that we ate our first salmon cookie.


Fast forward six years and we were living in our parents basement. We needed to do something. We remembered our vision quest and those funny cookies we ate. So, we made some. And, it turns out that with enough sugar, they aren't terrible. Now our cookies are available to you. Take some and embark on your vision quest. Or, just eat them to be unique. We don't care.
